# Trust Center

QUBUHUB builds intelligent systems for real-world use.

That means capability alone is not enough.

Our systems are designed around:
- truthful operation,
- bounded autonomy,
- user agency,
- safety by design,
- and accountable deployment.

## Governance
We publish our operational standards openly.

- AI Constitution
- Model Behavior Standard
- Agent Safety Policy
- Agent Terms
- Security Policy

## Model Family
Our current model family:

- **LMLM**

LMLM operates under the same constitutional and deployment standards as the broader QUBUHUB platform.

## Security
We support responsible disclosure and security research conducted in good faith.

## Transparency
We believe users and developers should understand:
- what our systems can do,
- what they cannot do,
- and how they are governed.
