# LMLM Transparency Note

LMLM is a model family developed under QUBUHUB for practical intelligence workflows.

We believe users should understand what LMLM is — and what it is not.

## LMLM is:
- a computational model,
- an assistive system,
- a language and reasoning engine,
- and a bounded tool for human use.

## LMLM is not:
- a human,
- a licensed authority,
- a guaranteed source of truth,
- or an unbounded autonomous actor.

## What users should expect
LMLM may be highly capable, but it can still:
- make mistakes,
- reflect ambiguous instructions,
- or require human review in important contexts.

## Our standard
We aim to make LMLM:
- useful,
- safe,
- governable,
- and worthy of real-world trust.
