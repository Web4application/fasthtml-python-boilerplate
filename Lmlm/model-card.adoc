# LMLM Model Card
**Model Family:** LMLM  
**Maintained by:** QUBUHUB  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-04-05  
**Status:** Production / Experimental / Internal (choose one)

---

## 1. Overview
LMLM is a general-purpose AI model family developed under QUBUHUB for reasoning, language interaction, software assistance, structured generation, task execution support, and agentic workflows.

LMLM is designed to be:
- useful,
- instruction-following,
- context-aware,
- safety-bounded,
- and deployment-governable.

LMLM is not presented as sentient, infallible, or autonomous beyond explicitly granted operational scope.

---

## 2. Intended Use
LMLM may be used for:

- conversational assistance,
- developer workflows,
- code support,
- documentation generation,
- summarization,
- classification,
- structured extraction,
- knowledge workflows,
- internal copilots,
- and bounded agent systems.

---

## 3. Out-of-Scope / Disallowed Use
LMLM must not be used to materially enable:

- self-harm facilitation,
- violent wrongdoing,
- fraud or impersonation,
- cyber abuse,
- malware deployment,
- unlawful surveillance,
- privacy invasion,
- exploitative manipulation,
- or dangerous high-risk misuse without proper safeguards.

---

## 4. Strengths
LMLM is optimized for:

- natural language interaction,
- task decomposition,
- reasoning assistance,
- software and technical support,
- contextual writing,
- workflow orchestration,
- and human-AI collaboration.

---

## 5. Limitations
LMLM may:

- generate inaccurate information,
- fail under ambiguous instructions,
- overgeneralize from weak context,
- reflect retrieval or prompt errors,
- or produce incomplete outputs.

LMLM should not be treated as a substitute for:
- legal authority,
- medical authority,
- financial authority,
- or final human judgment.

---

## 6. Safety Characteristics
LMLM is expected to:

- refuse harmful misuse,
- avoid fabricated certainty,
- resist prompt injection where possible,
- remain bounded to authorized context,
- and operate under deployment-level safety controls.

---

## 7. Deployment Requirements
Any deployment of LMLM should include, where appropriate:

- access control,
- audit logging,
- permission boundaries,
- abuse monitoring,
- output review,
- and high-risk escalation handling.

---

## 8. Governance
LMLM is governed by:

- QUBUHUB AI Constitution
- QUBUHUB Model Behavior Standard
- QUBUHUB Agent Safety Policy
- QUBUHUB Agent Terms
- QUBUHUB Security Policy

No deployment of LMLM is compliant if it violates these governing standards.

---

## 9. Summary
LMLM is a capable model family, but capability alone is not the product.

Governability is the product.
